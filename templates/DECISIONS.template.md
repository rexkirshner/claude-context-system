# Technical Decisions Log

This document records significant technical decisions made during development, including rationale, alternatives considered, and trade-offs.

## How to Use This Document

**When to add an entry:**
- Choosing frameworks or major libraries
- Architectural pattern decisions
- Technology stack choices
- Significant algorithm or approach decisions
- Major refactoring decisions

**Entry format:**
- What was decided
- Why it was decided
- What alternatives were considered
- Trade-offs accepted
- When to reconsider
